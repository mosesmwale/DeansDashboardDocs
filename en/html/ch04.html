Overview

The educational indicators to be tracked should be at a macro-level – dealing with program performance rather than of individual student/staff performance. The following key performance areas were identified to tracked on the Dean’s dashboard:

  1. Student selection, admission and numbers

  2. Student progression and achievement

  3. Student development support and guidance

  4. Teaching and learning

  5. Research

  6. Human Resources

  7. Budgeting and fiscal management

  8. Alumni (graduate tracking)
